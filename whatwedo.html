WHAT WE DO

We help position policies on government agendas to advance solutions to public health issues. We facilitate the inclusion of new and necessary vaccines on the immunization formulary of national and/or local governments. We expedite the upgrade of clinical guidelines to include new, more effective, and sometimes less-expensive drugs. We help position immunization, policy formulation, and other issues at the top of the agenda of national and international organizations. We match pipelines of health products and devices with countries' disease burdens. We know policy makers and those who influence them. We communicate and package information in a meaningful way for policy and decision makers.

Our work is performed by a 100% virtual network. We have consultants located in all continents, who are familiar with the issues and market conditions that impact the health system. Our virtual business model keeps projects ongoing in multiple time zones.

WHERE WE DO iT

We are truly global and really local. Our policy work and shaping abilities span all regions, from North, Central and South America, the Caribbean, Europe, the Middle East and Africa, to Asia and Australia. We shape policies in cities, provinces, states, countries, regions and globally. Our combined experience covers more than 150 countries around the world.

We maintain a strong core team of seasoned professionals in key areas who are familiar with the issues and market conditions that impact the health system. Through our business partnership approach, and through our consultants' use of our methodologies, we can provide our clients with local expertise and “feet on the ground” to work on the issues that impact their business in the specific countries in which they operate.
