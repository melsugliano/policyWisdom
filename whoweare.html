WHO WE ARE

ABOUT US

Policy Wisdom provides clients with the strategies, resources, and vision necessary to achieve their public-health policy goals. We shape public health policies by working with the full spectrum of individuals—from policy makers to those who guide their thinking—at governmental and non-governmental organizations and in the private sector. Policy Wisdom works in partnership with our clients to create a thoughtful and actionable strategic path to shape policies that impact the health, environment, safety, and development of individuals, communities, and nations. Through our partnership approach, we pay close attention to clients' goals and needs.

Policy Wisdom's ViSiON is to be a benchmark of excellence in health policy and a valued partner in the creation of policies that benefit public health worldwide.

Our MiSSiON is to create value for our clients by providing evidence-based solutions that have a positive impact on public health wherever we engage.

Policy Wisdom was founded in 2010 with the ambitious goal of playing a role in every worldwide public health initiative.

We have become a key partner to organizations, companies, and governments, and have completed numerous projects across the world that are directed toward seeking access for products and services that are beneficial to the well-being of individuals and society, and that encourage and accelerate innovation that improves the quality of life.

MEET OUR TEAM

Our team of passionate individuals works with our robust in-house developed proprietary tools, services, and technical methodologies, which are the backbone for the service we offer. They apply sound principles of research, advocacy, social marketing, and health communications, as well as their education and experience, to determine the approaches that will be most successful to shape policies. We offer our clients a seasoned staff with decades of combined public-health policy experience—experts in public health, research, medicine, pharmaceutical operations, market surveys, interviewing, health communications, and document preparation, among other areas of expertise. Our virtual team is located in various countries around the world, connected through technology, and selected for given projects based on their experience, education, and language capabilities.
